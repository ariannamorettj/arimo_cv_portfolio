About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Cultural Heritage in the Digital Ecosystem at the University of Bologna. Additionally, I work as a Python software developer at OpenCitations, focusing on automating metadata crosswalks and creating reusable workflows for exchanging differently modeled metadata in the cultural heritage domain, particularly in RDF serialization formats. I extend these research interests to Spoke 4 of the PNRR Extended Partnership 5 (CHANGES project).
